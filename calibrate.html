Build a calibration

Upload the calibration charts you printed on the Lab and scanned back in. This fits the film's response and gives you a .json the corrector can load. Color and B&W are independent — do whichever you want.

← Back to the corrector

How this works

  1. In the corrector's Advanced panel, download the calibration charts and print each one on the Lab with correction OFF.
  2. Scan or photograph the prints. Best results: scan all prints in a single pass, with the scanner's auto-color / auto-exposure turned off, and the whole chart (including its black corner squares) in frame.
  3. Upload the scans below and hit Build. Check the overlay to confirm the patches were located, then download the .json.
  4. Back in the corrector, open Advanced → Load calibration and pick your file.

Color calibration optional

Upload Color chart 1 and/or Color chart 2. One works; both (98 patches) fits the tone curve and color cast more accurately.
