How this works

  1. Download the charts below (or individually in the corrector's Advanced panel) and print each one on the Lab with correction OFF.
  2. Scan or photograph the prints. Best results: scan all prints in a single pass, with the scanner's auto-color / auto-exposure turned off, and the whole chart (including its black corner squares) in frame.
  3. Upload the scans below and hit Build. Check the overlay to confirm the patches were located, then download the .json.
  4. Back in the corrector, open Advanced → Load calibration and pick your file.

All 8 charts in one file: 2 color + 1 B&W calibration charts, plus 5 refinement test charts (color cube ×2, extreme tone, repeatability, vignette).
